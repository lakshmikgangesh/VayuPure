TACKLE INDOOR AIRBONE DISEASES
We aim to develop a strategy to suppress indoor air-transmission of diseases such as COVID-19 and TB.
India centric solution
Designing with India in mind, we seek to create a low cost and robust strategy of air disinfection.
Effective Purification
By using a mixture of ventilation, disinfection, and filtering we plan to provide a complete air purification solution.
It is estimated that around 40% of Indian population is infected by Tuberculosis, considering the population of India it is a large number, the situation gets even grim after the offset of COVID-19 pandemic.

The indoor confined space is one of the reasons for the spread of such viruses. To address the issue of indoor air filtration, IIT Madras, QMUL, VIT and Magneto cleantech have come up with an idea Vayu-pure, to deliver an experimental proof of concept of a revolutionary air filter to deal with air-borne pathogens considering heavy pollution settings in the India.
