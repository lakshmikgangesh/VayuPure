VayuPure
We aim to develop an air purification system that would effectively
eliminate air-borne pathogens, by virtue of a international collaboration
and solution-oriented approach within the ethics of scientific research.
TACKLE INDOOR AIRBONE DISEASES
We aim to develop a strategy to suppress indoor
air-transmission of diseases such as COVID-19 and TB.
India centric solution
Designing with India in mind, we seek to create a low
cost and robust strategy of air disinfection.
Effective Purification
By using a mixture of ventilation, disinfection, and filtering
we plan to provide a complete air purification solution.
