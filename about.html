Our Goals

As of now, we have set 6 major goals for ourselves
in this project.
Categorizing the typical ventilation in domestic indoor
condition using existing data and develop a strategy based
on CO2 monitoring.
Development of low cost and robust product for indoor air disinfection.
To promote multidisciplinary and multi institutional research.
Encouraging collaboration in between industry and academia.
Enlarging the sphere of common understanding by inviting more voices from different sectors of IAQ community.
